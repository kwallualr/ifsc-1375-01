Tesla

About

Tesla is a company that creates cars that are completely electric. Tesla cars help the environment because you don't use gas to keep them running. Tesla cars have autopilot on every car. The autopilot the car comes with will just drive your car in one lane. You can buy a better autopilot that will change lanes for you and more. Tesla's have different features in the cars. There is a setting that allows you to leave your animals in a fully air conditioned car while still remaining to be locked. You can play all sorts of games and you get access to all sorts of apps.

Model Descriptions

Model 3
The Model 3 is the cheapest Tesla. It is a pretty average car.
Model S
The Model S is the second fastest Tesla. It is very sleak and looks very nice.
Model X
The Model X is one of the SUV type Teslas. The Model X has falcon wings and is fast for a SUV.
Model Y
The Model Y is the cheapest SUV. It is pretty average for an SUV.
Roadster
The Roadster is the fastest vehicle. I think it could be considered a supercar.
Cybertruck
The Cybertruck is a truck. More of an SUV, but a truck nonetheless. It is supposed to be unbreakable, but it has yet to come out.

Ranked List (in my opinion)

  1. Roadster - it is a fast car and sleak
  2. Model S - it is the second fastest car and looks really nice
  3. Cybertruck - it is kind of ugly, but I dig it
  4. Model X - it has falcon wings and is still fast
  5. Model Y - it is brand new and it is very spacious
  6. Model 3 - it is the cheapest and very common
