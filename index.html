Thoughts on Web Accessibility

Web Accessibility means making your website easier for people with disabilities. It is important to build accessible websites because absolutely anybody would be able to use your website. Blind people and people who have mobility problems would be able to access to work through your website.

Thoughts on Progreesive Enhancement

Progressive enhancement is focuses on the content when considering how well a website works. Progressive enhancement is important because of how it considers how well your site works. It helps you go through the steps of figuring out your website and working out any kinks along the way.
